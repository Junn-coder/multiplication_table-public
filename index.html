Learn Multiplication Table v1.0
Purpose

This program helps kids master multiplication tables. It works best when parents practice together with kids at the beginning. This program is free and ad-free. The app is kid-friendly and focuses on practice.

Usage
  1. Pick a number on the left.
  2. Use Study to read, view the apples, and fill the practice box.
  3. Labels appear briefly; hover the image to see row, column, and product again.
  4. Tap Speak, listen to "a times b equals", then say the answer word.
  5. Use Test to answer questions and then show results.
  6. Open Progress to see what needs more practice.
  7. Use Feedback to share a rating and comment.
